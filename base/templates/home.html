A little about us!

Open Air Tirana is a Outdoor Cinema playing best classic movies.
If you are in the city, you can enjoy a movie under the stars !!
You can bring snacks to the movie which you can get from our bar, and if you think the party is over after the movie, you are wrong. Our DJ will play music for you until the late hours of the night. So what are you waiting for? Come spend a wonderful evening with us!
